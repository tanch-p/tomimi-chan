The UI is designed to be similar to as in-game so you can expect the areas that are interactive in game and visible here to have similar functions

The following UI options (enemy stat related) are available:

  • Selection of Relics (accessed by clicking on Relic Icon at bottom left corner)
  • Emergency Ops Toggle
  • Selection of Difficulty
  • Selection of Current Floor (accessed by clicking on Floor Title at the top)
  • Selection of Collapsal Modes (same as above)
